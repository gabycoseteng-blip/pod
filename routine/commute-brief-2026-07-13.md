## SEGMENT 4 — VOCAB OF THE DAY (in-language; 2 Mandarin HSK4, 2 Tagalog)
- **局势 (júshì)** — situation / state of affairs (esp. political/military).
  书面语/formal. Contrast 情况 (general circumstances, informal) and 形势
  (overall trend). Ties to the Hormuz/Iran 局势.
- **难免 (nánmiǎn)** — inevitably / hard to avoid (advanced connective-adverb,
  slightly resigned tone; 难免会…). Contrast 必然 (logical inevitability, harder).
  Ties to: once Hormuz shuts, higher oil is 难免.
- **pananalasa** — the ravaging / onslaught of a destructive force (a storm, a
  crisis). Contrast **pinsala** (the resulting damage). Ties to the typhoon/habagat.
- **kabuhayan** — livelihood / whole economic means of living (broader than
  **hanapbuhay**, a job/occupation). Ties to the wage hike, cost of living,
  remittances.

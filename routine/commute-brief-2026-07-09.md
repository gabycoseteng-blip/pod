## VOCAB OF THE DAY (2 Mandarin, then 2 Tagalog — all NEW, in-language)
Already used (do NOT reuse): 谈判, 电网, 反而, 稳定 | alimpuyo, sapantaha, habagat, ginhawa.
1. **一旦 (yīdàn)** — "once / in the event that (X happens, then…)". HSK-4 advanced connective; conditional-temporal, formal/书面语. Ties to Iran/oil: 一旦…就… — once Hormuz is threatened, oil jumps. Contrast vs 如果 (neutral "if"): 一旦 = once it actually happens, the consequence follows and it's weightier/less hypothetical.
2. **估计 (gūjì)** — "to estimate / reckon". HSK-4, abstract. Ties to markets estimating the Fed path / earnings estimates. Register nuance: 估计 leans 口语 (an everyday guess/hunch); the finance desk's formal word is **预计 (yùjì)**, "to forecast/project" (书面语). Same idea, different register.
3. **pasanin** — "a burden / load one shoulders" (root *pasan*, to carry on the shoulders/back). Ties to the oil-import bill and ballooning trade deficit as a burden on the peso and households. Near-synonym vs **pabigat** (an added dead-weight that drags things down): *pasanin* is the responsibility you take on and carry; *pabigat* is extra weight that makes everything heavier.
4. **sigwa** — "a storm / squall / tempest," and figuratively upheaval or turmoil (*sigwa ng damdamin*, a storm of emotion; political *sigwa*). Ties to Super Typhoon Inday + the geopolitical/market *sigwa*. Contrast vs **bagyo** (the literal meteorological typhoon): *sigwa* is the squall/tempest and carries the figurative, more literary sense *bagyo* doesn't.
